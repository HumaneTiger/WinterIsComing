Earth has been hit by a devastating magnetic solar storm that occurs only every few hundred years.

All electrical and electronic equipment has been destroyed, the infrastructure is unusable for decades, and human society teeters on the precipice of its existence.

A couple has managed to escape from their city before all order has collapsed. Stranded in the wilderness, they must gather enough supplies and build a suitable shelter until winter.

Unfortunately, they lack the necessary knowledge and tools. Both can only be found in a nearby town - a race against time and danger begins...
